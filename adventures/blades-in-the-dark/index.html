Blades
In The Dark

By Chris Hart

Time slot: 12 Hours

Want to join a crew of daring scoundrels seeking their fortune on the haunted streets of an industrial fantasy city. There are heists, chases, occult mysteries, dangerous bargains, bloody skirmishes, and, above all, riches to be had - if you’re bold enough to seize them.

You and your fledgeling crew must thrive amidst the threats of rival gangs, powerful noble families, vengeful ghosts, the Bluecoats of the city watch and the siren song of your own scoundrel’s vices. Will you rise to power in the underworld? What are you willing to do to get to the top?

Peaky Blinders with swords and ghosts! What’s not to like? You’ll be on the hunt for a list of items chosen by sponsors. Will you find them before the night is out?
