About

It's that time of year again!
This year we're raising money for Alder Hey Children's Hospital.

Also in a change from previos events, we plan on running 4 different games simultaneously! As always these will be run for a full 24 hours needing to be carefully run (despite lack of sleep).

The players will also bid/donate real money to take part, with the highest bidders/donators taking a place at one of the tables. It will need support refs as well as support from as many people as possible to make it as unique, special and worthwhile as possible.

On the day, if it is like our previous attempts, there will no doubt be a running commentary, and photos to show the depths of stress we can get into as the night continues.

So, despite the fun I hope we'll have, it will most definitely be a feat of endurance, logistics, and skill.

How to Get involved

There are two methods by which you can get involved:

The first way you can get involved is by sponsering the poor saps who are roleplay for the 24 hour period. To sponser us please donate to our just giving page: https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/24-hour-charity-rpg-marathon.

The second method you can get involved is by bidding to be a player in one of the campaigns. You can put yourself forward to play by emailing to kinoandhermes@gmail.com. You can find out more about the campaigns being run below.
